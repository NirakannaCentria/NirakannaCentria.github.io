Who am I?

I'm Anna, a mother to 2 girls, a wife, a friend, a daughter, a sister, a worker and student. I have just finished my second year at Centria University of Applied Sciences, and I hope to graduate within 2-3 years with a Bachelors degree in IT Engineering. While I am conducting most of my studies in evenings and during the weekends I spend my daytime at a local company working as a System developer mainly focusing on the company's ERP system.

If I have any free time left after working and studying I like to go swimming and I also go to the gym. I love to bake, spend time in nature and to read. When I graduate I will get one or five dogs depending on what I can convince the rest of the family to accept
